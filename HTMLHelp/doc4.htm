FAQ

The most common answers to the usage of PYUV.

  1. What is exactly the licence adopted by PYUV?
    It is not clear to me, too... but let's say that you can safely use it for your own research, even of commercial and profit nature, and I will not ask you for money or acknowledgements (which, of course, are anyway much appreciated!).

  2. How do I get the source code?
    I have not yet defined a policy/licence for releasing the source code, so it is not available now.

  3. I have not a clear vision of the raw format matter: how do I improve it?
    The answer is simple: browse Wikipedia.
